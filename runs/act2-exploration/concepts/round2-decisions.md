## Decision Rationale

**Transition: `transition_pulled_in.png`**
Selected over `transition_warped_zoom.png` so the AI companion remains visibly in the shot. The companion's arm reaching out of the terminal grabbing Sean's hand keeps the character relationship continuous through the transition rather than abstracting to a vortex.

**Revelation: `revelation.png` (original)**
Selected over `revelation_v2_dissolving`, `revelation_v3_radiating`, and `revelation_v4_dreamspace`. The original revelation flows better narratively with the preceding "pulled into terminal" transition — Sean and the companion reaching across the opening world is a natural continuation of the hand-grab moment, while the alternates would require a harder cut.

**Final Panorama: `final_panorama_v3_a.png`**
Selected over `_v3_b` and `_v3_c`. v3_a was the cleanest variant of the cleaned panorama with Sean's body in 3/4 toward camera (the v1 craftsman pose) and the agentic workflow theme content (AGENT HARNESS, TOOL USE, AI AGENTS RUN WORKFLOWS, AGENT → TOOL → RESULT diagram).
