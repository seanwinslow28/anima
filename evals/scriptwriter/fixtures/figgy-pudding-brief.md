# Studio Brief — "Figgy Pudding" (eval voice exemplar, synthetic)

*Not an anima production — the Sean-supplied premise from the 2026-06-15
screenwriting-voice dry-run, captured as the dialogue-heavy voice case. Cast:
Greta + Doug (`greta` / `doug` namespaces), distinct from the anima pencil-test
cast so the beat sheet round-trips in isolation against its own namespaces.*

## What is this story about?

Christmastime. A couple — Greta and Doug, 40s, a marriage gone to dead air — eats
a poorly cooked dinner. Carolers arrive singing "We Wish You a Merry Christmas."
The "bring us a figgy pudding / we won't go until we get some" lines curdle into a
demand. The couple retreats inside; the carolers won't leave. That night a hard
fig smashes through the window; the carolers are still out there. The couple
finally clocks the lyric and scrambles to make a figgy pudding to get the carolers
out of their lives.

The WHAT: two people who've quietly quit on their marriage get conscripted into the
kitchen together by a dessert-based home invasion — and the dirty victory is that,
for one freezing wrecked-window hour, they're on the same side and don't hate it.
The figgy pudding is the stupid thing they seize control of because they control
nothing else. The win is cooperation, clawed — never spoken.

## Tone

Comedy with thriller sprinkled in. Warmth under the darkness; the thaw earned only
through action, never narrated. Dirty-victory + callback closer; the theme
("marriage") never stated aloud.

## Who is the cast?

- **Greta** (`greta`) — wired, flailing, talks to manage panic.
- **Doug** (`doug`) — the immovable deadpan wall; terminal monosyllables.
