INT. MAHONEY KITCHEN — NIGHT

Greta claws through a cabinet like a raccoon working a dumpster.

                    GRETA
          It's a pudding, it has figs, the name is the recipe,
          this is the one dessert that tells you how to make it
          while you're being threatened by it —

The defect: the final clause is the writer reaching over Greta's shoulder to make
a tidy joke about the premise's own dramatic irony. A woman actually under siege
keeps clawing the cabinet; she doesn't compose a clean line about it.
