# Studio Brief — Pencil Test Act 1 (fixture)

A reproduction Studio Brief drawn from the shipped Pencil Test Act 1 reference.
Used by evals/planner/ to exercise Maya against a known-good baseline.

## What is this story about?

A solo animator at his desk, drawing on a tablet. The character he just drew
lifts his stylus off the page and watches him. The piece is about that quiet
recognition between maker and made.

## Who is this character?

Sean — mid-thirties, lean build, broad shoulders. Sandy-blonde hair, soft jaw.
Wears dark navy shirt, cool gray pants. Stylus in his right hand. Always.

## What is the tone?

Quiet, melancholy, recognizing. Adult, not childlike.

### What this is NOT

- Pencil-test rough, not glossy.
- Melancholic, not grieving.
- Not anime. Not 3D-rendered. Not motion-graphics-clean.

## What is the format?

60-second portfolio loop. Two acts. Act 1 shipped; Act 2 in flight.

## What is the target medium?

GIF on the portfolio page, with WebM + MP4 archival masters.

## What is the deadline?

Hard: 2026-06-30. Soft: 2026-06-15.

## What are the non-negotiables?

- Stylus stays in right hand, every frame.
- Cream paper texture visible in every frame.
- 16:9 aspect ratio within 2% tolerance.
- Character remains recognizably Sean across every frame.
